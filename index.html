This is how I work:

  1. Understand the problem: Include as many users, or people who truly understand the user as possible and flesh out the user's goals.
  2. Understand the current solutions: Are users currently using a different app to solve this problem? Are they using an excel spreadsheet? Are they using a pen and paper?
  3. Ideation and sketching: Diverge and explore as many different solutions to the problem as possible.
  4. Prototyping: Pick the best ideas, or a combination of the best ideas, and present them in a fidelity high enough to understand how the interface will work.
  5. Testing: Show the prototype to as many users, or people who truly understand the users as possible. Gather feedback. Iterate. Iterate. Iterate.
  6. Implementation: Build the damn thing.
