Everything you need to know about the new

Content Optimization System

(COS for short)

What is the COS?

The Content Optimization System is the first integrated content system to recognize the unique needs of visitors and optimize content for each viewer. It is mobile-optimized out-of-the-box, fully integrated with every marketing channel, and adaptive to the changing needs of leads and customers. With the COS, people become more than anonymous pageviews.

What's Changed?

Landing Pages

In the new HubSpot Landing Pages you’ll find:

  • Out of the box mobile optimization through responsive design.
  • Progressive Profiling and Smart Fields for your forms.
  • Drag and Drop template builder and WYSWIG content editor.
  • Easy social share toggle buttons and integrated tracking for sharing your page.
  • Smart Content and tokens for personalizing content to each viewer.
  • Sophisticated submission follow-up emails and integration with workflows.

Blogging Tools

In the new Blogging Software you’ll find:

  • Out of the box mobile optimization through responsive design.
  • New keywords integration and author tags for modern search engine optimization.
  • Customizable author pages for each of your bloggers.
  • An easy to build and modify template builder.

Website Pages

If you use the HubSpot COS to host your website you’ll get:

  • Out of the box mobile optimization through responsive design.
  • Drag and Drop template builder and WYSWIG content editor.
  • Smart Content and tokens for personalizing content to each viewer.
  • One integrated system for all your marketing tools.
  • A fast and secure content delivery network.

We're here to help.

One thing that hasn’t changed is the support and training that HubSpot provides to all of our customers, particularly around learning new tools. You can sign up for Academy Classes to learn these new tools, read more about them in the help docs, and always reach out to us through the Need Help Tab at the bottom of this page when you get stuck.

How do I get it?

These new tools enhance major parts of your overall marketing strategy. Because of the critical importance of your website, landing pages and blog, we have laid out a series of recommended steps tailored to your company to help you determine your readiness and set a plan to move onto the new platform. These are exciting updates, but there’s also no rush here, move when you’re ready. We’ll be here every step of the way to help.
