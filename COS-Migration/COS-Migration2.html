Other Options:

Option 1Have technical skills? You can move your pages on your own.

If you’d prefer, you can get certified through one of our migration classes and move your own pages. Moving your own pages means you can start whenever you’d like, even before 2014, but it does take some technical know-how and time.

Get Certified

Option 2Get a fresh start: Build an entirely new website or blog.

Sometimes you need a fresh start. If you don’t have content you’d like to move, you can get started building a brand new blog or website now. We can walk you through the content set-up process and connect you to the right training classes to get the most out of your new software.

Start Building

Option 3Move your existing content to the COS, plus start on the COS website or blog

If you really see the potential of the COS and would like to use it for your website or other content too, you can request that HubSpot move your existing non-HubSpot website or blogs to the COS. We will show you how your site could look on the COS and walk you through that migration process. If you do have HubSpot migrate your site, there will be a $10/page fee. Alternately, if you’re highly technical, you could opt to build your site on the COS yourself.

Schedule A Time

Option 4Push the pause button: Don’t make any moves just yet.

Need time to think about it? Busy time of the year for you? We get it. Choose this option to hold off on making any decisions just yet and continue using your existing tools. We’ll remind you several months from now to take a look again. While you’re waiting, why not get the some training classes?
